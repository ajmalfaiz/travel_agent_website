Dubai
8 Nights 7 Days

If you are looking for a unique destination that is both a dynamic business centre and a tourist paradise, look no further. Dubai is just the place for you. Offering several attractions, shopping and fine dining in quality hotels, Dubai is indeed a wonder to behold. Suburban offers 8 days tour package from Cochin to enjoy your holidays in Dubai. From the timeless tranquility of the desert to the lively bustle of the souk, Dubai offers a kaleidoscope of attractions for visitors. The emirate embraces a stunning variety of scenery in a very small area. You can experience everything from sand dunes and rugged mountains to beaches and lush green parks. From dusty villages to luxurious residential districts and from ancient houses with wind towers to ultra-modern shopping malls. Dubai is a laid-back tourist escape; a city with the simplicity of a bygone era; a cosmopolitan society with an international lifestyle with the unique charm and hospitality of Arabia.

Day 1: Dubai, Arrive at Dubai Airport or Sharjah Airport. You will have priority clearence through immigration procedures and will be assisted by our representative. Our representatives will also assist you with your baggage and customs formalities. You will then be transferred to the hotel in Dubai City and checked-in. Relax and refresh yourself. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Dubai, After breakfast depart from hotel and proceed for half day city tour and return to hotel. At around 3PM, we head out on an extremely amazing Dubai Evening DESERT SAFARI. Return to the hotel for the night stay.

Day 3: Dubai, After breakfast, check out of the hotel. Our next destination is the East Coast of the UAE, known as Khorfakken. See various beautiful landscapes of the UAE in one day. This tour will take us away from the hustle and bustle of the city. We pass the lush green agricultural area of Dhaid before reaching the Hajar Dubai Travels Holidays mountains, where we stop at a local market .This is where locally made pottery, fruits and carpets are sold at bargain prices. From masafi we start our round trip on the East Coast. Driving through mountains we will pass wadis-large and small, which are dried out riverbeds formed during milleniums. As we reach the Indian Ocean we will make a short trip to Omani territory as well. At Dibba we visit a local fish market with its abundant and colourful catch. Driving along the coast through small fishing villages, we will arrive to Bidaya Mosque, the oldest one in the UAE. Our trip continues towards Khorfakkan- One of the most popular place for Scuba diving and Snorkeling. The day ends as you spend the night stay at Hotel

Day 4: Hatta, Following breakfast, we check out of the hotel and depart to Hatta, which is the mountainous region of Dubai. On the way, we pass the Bidaya Mosque and pass through the Emirate of Fujairah, where we will visit the Fujairah Fort. We pass through the city of Kalba before driving through Wadis(Natural Pools)as we arrive in Hatta. In Hatta,we visit the Hatta village and Hatta fort. After that, we check-in at hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5: Al Ain, Have breakfast before checking out from the Hatta hotel and departing to Al-Ain. Al-Ain , known as the Garden City, is one of the oldest settlements in the region. It is a stunning green city with an incredible number of public parks and numerous green houses. Al-Ain is one of the agricultural centers of the emirates and also a university town. It is the birthplace of the president of UAE. Our first visit, on arrival to this oasis town surrounded by red sand, is the most important musuem in the UAE where you can see artifacts from a famous excavation site nearby , ancient Arabic manuscript and royal treasures. Next is a trip to the quaint camel market where you have a good oppurtunity to observe these extraordinary "ships of desert". We continue the tour with a visit to the nearby oasis village of Buraimi at Omani territory, where we will also have lunch. The village is an exciting experience with its old forts, date palm groves and 2000 year old irrigation systems called Falaj, which are still in use. Afterwards we proceed to Hili Gardens, the excavation site of a tomb dating back 3000 B C. and other archeological discoveries. The day ends as you spend the night at a hotel in Al Ain.

Day 6: Abu Dhabi, Today we will depart to the capital of the United Arab Emirates, Abu Dhabi. This tour is the ideal way to discover the beautiful capital of Abu Dhabi. On arrival to this center of oil business and administration, we visit the petroleum Exhibition where we are shown a film about the amazing development in Abu Dhabi during last 30 years. Afterwards we will stop at a carpet market where a huge variety of fabulous carpets from all around the world are sold. Our tour continues to the old Souk where you can experience the bustle of everyday life and the magnificient Abu Dhabi Corniche up to the end of the breakwater that stretches out in to the sea, giving you a splended view of the city skyline. Our tour ends with a short round at the yard where traditional boats are built. Then we check in to a Abu Dhabi hotel for the night stay.

Day 7: Dubai, We check out of the Abu Dhabi hotel after breakfast and depart to Dubai, where we will check in to a hotel. You are free to decide the schedule for the rest of the day. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 8: Departure Dubai, After breakfast at the hotel, you will be transferred to the Dubai Airport or the Sharjah Airport for your departure to homeland with lot of beautiful memories from Dubai - The City of Gold!
