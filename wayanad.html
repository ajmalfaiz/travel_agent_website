Wayanad
2 Nights 3 Days

Wayanad is the most important tourist destination in North Kerala. Your holidays in Kerala can be delightfully enjoyed with this 3 days Wayanad tour package starting from Calicut. The package covers all the major tourist attractions of Wayanad. It is the ideal location for adventure tourism as well as eco - tourism. Wayanad tour package covers all the major sightseeing destinations of Wayanad.

Day 1: Your Wayanad trip starts on your arrival at Calicut airport or railway station. Our representative/chauffeur will welcome you at the arrival terminal/gate and you will be driven directly to Resort. On the way you will be delighted with the mesmerizing sight of hills, lakes reflecting the surrounding lustrous forests, caves, small streams and miniature waterfalls. On your arrival you are cordially led into the leisure of a hotel room. Dinner and the overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Morning after a healthy breakfast from the hotel proceed for full day sightseeing in Wayanad. A trek up the Ambukuthi hill near Ambalavayal takes you to the neolithic Cave site of Edakkal. Later visit Muthanga forest in the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, where you watch herds of wild Elephants and other mammals and birds. After having your lunchproceed to Kuruva Island. This beautiful Island occupies in 955 acres of green forest on the tributaries of east flowing river Kabani. After roaming around you will be driven to back to your hotel. Enjoy the Dinner and overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 3: Check-out the hotel after the breakfast and proceed to Vythiri which is just 12 kms from Kalpetta. Visit Pookode lake, the enduring fresh water lake nestled among wooded hills which is 3 kms south of Vythiri. You can also enjoy boating in the lake. Later we drive you back to the airport/railway station for your onward journey.
