Sri Lanka
3 Days 2 Nights

3 Days Sri Lanka tour package from Cochin gives you an excellent oppurtunity to explore the natural beauty of this beautiful Island. Sri Lanka Ayubowan means "Welcome" (Sinhalese way of welcoming) .Sri Lanka is blessed with year-long sunshine and serene sandy beaches. You can choose calm waters of steady surf in this idyllic island.Imagine shooting the skies in a Hot Air Balloon ride, gliding weightless on top of the world! Share this moment with your dear ones for an unforgettable experience. Prominent landmarks of this country can be viewed beautifully.Sri Lanka is immersed in heritage and it's history rewinds over 2,000 years. Few of Asia’s best preserved monuments including UNESCO World Heritage sites are the main attractions. Age old heritage lives on in the form of cultural practice and traditional beliefs which comprises Sri Lankan society. Basic religions are Buddhism and Hinduism while the Dutch, Portugese and British interacted with Malay, Chinese and Arab have imparted their customs, cuisine & architecture. This is a land of continuousfestivities. Adventurers are spoilt on endless thrills like – cricket, golf, surfing, paragliding, canoeing, cycling, backpacking, camping or trekking.

Day 01 : Airport -Kandy, OYour Sri Lanka holidays package starts on your arrival at the Srilanka airport. Meet our tour representative who will escort you to Kandy. Kandy is a Royal city, sheltered by the hills and looped by the Mahaweli- Srilanka's longest river. It still holds religious and cultural significance to the people. Kandy is referred as the World Heritage City. Visit the famous Peradeniya Botanical Gardens - first built as a pleasure garden by Sinhala King and expanded by the British. It has around 147 acres in extent and provides an amazing variety of trees,plants & colourfull flowers. A stroll around the sleepy , but serene Kandy Lake, where balmy winds waft across, a visit to the University of Peradeniya-the spacious green campus laid out near the banks of the Mahaweli. It is a must watch for any visitor to Kandy. Visit the Kandy town and bazaar, the arts & crafts center, a gem musuem and a lapidary. In the evening, visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and view a traditional dance performance. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 02 : Kandy-Colombo, After breakfast, proceed to Colombo.En-route visit Elephant Orphanage in Pinnewala. Pinnewala, the Elephant Orphanage located on the banks of the Maha Oya, is mainly to house young elephants who find themselves displaced and lost from their natural habitats for various reasons-environmental changes due to development projects , being abandoned by their parents when they fall into pits and ravines,etc. Visit during feeding or bathing time of elephants is interesting.Then proceed to Colombo and do a city/shopping tour of Colombo - the capital of Sri Lanka. Spread along the shore of Indian Ocean, Colombo is the business and commercial hub of Sri Lanka. A drive through the city will take you to Colombo's most fashionable residential district -- the Cinnamon Gardens and Pettah, Colombo's busiest and most traditional bazaar area. Visit the Hindu Temple with elaborate stone carvings, the Dutch church at Wolfendhal dating back to 1749, the historic Devatagaha mosque and observe the old parliament, the BMICH(Bandaarnaike Memorial International Conference Hall). Also, visit the ceramics corporation showroom, Majestic City Shopping Complex, House of Fashion and the Odel unlimited dress boutique. Thereafter, overnight stay at hotel.

Day 03 : Colombo -Airport, After breakfast, depart to Colombo International airport with unforgetable refreshing moments to board your flight to the onward destination.
