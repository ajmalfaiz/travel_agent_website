Malaysia
3 Nights 4 Days

Malaysia is a gem of Southeast Asia. Situated on the Malay Peninsula, it is a bubbling and bustling melting point for the different races and religion, Malay, Indian, Chinese and many other ethnic groups who live here in peace and happiness. Multiculturalism has made this country a gastronomical heaven and home to many colourful festivals and events. Malaysia flaunts its bustling cities, tea plantation, lavish shopping malls, lush green gardens and eclectic mix of vintage colonial architecture.
Malaysia’s prime attraction is its extreme disparity which also adds colour to its theme of diversity. Notably tall buildings look down upon wooden houses build on stilts while luxury hotels sit just metres away from old reefs. Sky touching high mountains add further charm to rainforest-clad slopes sweeping down to floodplains teeming with forest life. Besides that, our Malaysia holiday package gives you an opportunity to get enthralled by the white sand beaches of famous west coast islands such as Pulau Redang and Pulau Langkawi, along with the lush rainforests of Borneo in the east.
Come and experience this beautiful country with our 3 nights 4 days Malaysia tour package.

Day 1: Upon arrival at the Kuala Lumpur airport, heads directly to your hotel. After completing all the check-in formalities take rest for a few hours. Later embark on a half-day Malaysia city tour. The first sightseeing of the day will be the Kings Palace. The official residence of the Yang di-Pertuan Agong, the monarch of Malaysia, it is a majestic building with impressive architecture. Next, move further to the National Monument which a sculpture that honours those who died in Malaysia's struggle for freedom, principally World War II and the Malayan Emergency. Next place on your Malaysia itinerary is Independence Square. Also known as Merdeka Square, it plays an important role in the Malayan history as the Malayan flag was hoisted here for the first time on 31 August 1957. Next, visit Beryl’s Chocolate House which is a place not less than a paradise for chocolate and coffee lovers. On your way back to hotel take some time to have a small photo session in front of magnificent Petronas Twin Towers. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: After breakfast on your Malaysia tour package, enjoy an exciting day trip to the Sunway Lagoon. Jam-packed with more than 80 rides and three theme lands, it is one of the most visited destinations in Malaysia. At the water park, you will find the world biggest man-made beach, the flow rider, a wave pool, a 360¬degree revolving pirate ship and Malaysia's first surf simulator. Other famous rides include the African pythons ride, six-lane congo challenge slide and the Cameroon climb. The attractions do not just end here, there is also the Interactive Scream Park and the Amusement Park, Wild West and World of Adventure zones with Extreme Park for adventure junkies, Wildlife Park and the first Nickelodeon-themed in Asia. After an adventure and fun-filled day, head towards your hotel in Kuala Lumpur for an overnight stay.

Day 3: In the morning, head for the Genting Highland day trip. The trip from Kuala Lumpur will take you through some picturesque places such as Batu Caves and the Titiwangsa Mountains. The first stopover will be Batu Cave which is famous for world’s tallest status of God Murugan, an Indian Deity. It is one of the top tourist attractions in Malaysia and is home to various beautiful caves and cave temples which are about 400 meters long and 100 meters high. Situated about 6,000 feet above sea level, Genting Highlands has Malaysia’s only casino resort and colossal theme parks. To reach here, take a skyway cable car ride amidst the spectacular Malayan rainforests. Upon reaching on the top of the hill, visit Casino de Genting, Malaysia’s only international casino resort. Afterward, explore more recreational facilities available here. Go for skydiving simulator at the sky venture and try other thrilling rides at the resort’s theme parks. In the evening, return back to your hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

Day 4: After breakfast, check-out from to the hotel and proceed to the airport with the good memories of Malaysia tour.
